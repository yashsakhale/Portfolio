AI Engineer crafting production-grade AI systems, deep learning architectures, and GenAI applications.

I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University with an M.S in Artificial Intelligence, Mechanical Engineering.

Professional Experience: I started my journey as an AI Intern at Vigilant Inc., a fast-paced startup in Alexandria, Virginia. From day one, I built core AI components from scratch: designing, developing, and deploying production-grade systems. My rapid impact led to my transition into a full-time AI Engineer role, where I continue to architect and scale advanced AI systems.

Academic Research: At CMU's CERLAB, I worked on simulating and modeling LiDAR sensors, utilizing camera-lidar fusion and predictive models to filter dust particles from point cloud data for autonomous vehicles on construction and mining sites.
